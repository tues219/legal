Privacy Policy

150 (the “App”) · Effective June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. The App is operated by DentCloud Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”). We respect your privacy and have built the App so that your health data stays on your device.

The short version

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal or health data. We operate no servers, use no analytics, and include no advertising or third‑party tracking. Everything the App computes happens on your own device.

Health data

With your permission, the App reads heart rate and resting heart rate from Apple Health (HealthKit) to calculate your weekly active minutes. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to us or to any third party. We do not use Health data for advertising or marketing, and we never share or sell it. You can review or revoke the App’s Health permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or in iOS/watchOS Settings.

Where your data lives

Calculated results and your settings are stored locally on your device (including a shared app group so the App, its widgets, and the Apple Watch app and complication can display your progress). Your settings are synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch directly via Apple’s WatchConnectivity framework. This information does not pass through any server operated by us.

Purchases

The App offers an optional one‑time “150 Pro” unlock. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple’s handling of purchase data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
